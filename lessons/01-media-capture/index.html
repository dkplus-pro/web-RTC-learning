What to notice

  • Permission: browser chrome owns the prompt; your UI explains intent.
  • Stream shape: a stream is a container; audio and video arrive as separate tracks.
  • Settings: the actual camera resolution may differ from what you requested.
  • Teardown: `track.stop()` releases hardware and ends the track.
